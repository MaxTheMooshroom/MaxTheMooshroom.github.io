### Ecosystem Simulation
I plan on creating a food web with various plants and animals that have traits similar to real-life counterparts in an attempt to create a statistical analysis of what it takes to create a sustainable habitat for long term survival, and what impacts human activities have on that stability. Current Progress: Studying underlying concepts. 
* {X} [Terrain Generation](https://github.com/MaxTheMooshroom/perlin_flight)
* {O} Realistic Terrain Erosion
* {O} Biome mapping with a realistic logical heatmap
* {O} Weather Patterns
* {O} Simulating Animal Behavior in a Controlled Environment
* {O} Simulating Plant Behavior in a Controlled Environment
